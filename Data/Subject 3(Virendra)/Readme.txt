The folders finger, openclose, palm and relaxed contain rectified data of the muscle group Flexor Digitorum Profundus.
Featurs such as mean average value(MAV), root mean square(RMS),varience(VAR),standard deviation(SD) and peak value(MAX) are extrcated stored in respective excel files.
Features of all the gestures are then combined in the order openclose,palm,relaxed and finger into the file vir_feat.
